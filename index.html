🧼 Meaning of Sanitization

Sanitization means the process of cleaning and removing dirt, germs, and harmful bacteria from surroundings, objects, or places to make them safe and hygienic.

👉 In simple words:

Sanitization = Cleaning to remove germs and keep things safe

🧠 Easy Examples:

  • Cleaning toilets with disinfectant
  • Washing hands with soap
  • Cleaning classrooms and desks
  • Sanitizing hospitals and public places
  • Using sanitizer in daily life

🌟 Why Sanitization is Important:

  • Prevents spread of diseases
  • Keeps environment clean and safe
  • Protects children and elderly people
  • Improves public health
