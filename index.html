Hygiene Connect & Sanitization Care

A community service project focusing on awareness of cleanliness, hygiene habits, and disease prevention in society.

🧠 Awareness & Guidelines

Hygiene means maintaining cleanliness to prevent diseases.

✔ Do’s

  • Wash hands before eating food
  • Use soap or sanitizer regularly
  • Keep surroundings clean
  • Dispose waste in dustbins only
  • Wear clean clothes daily

❌ Don’ts

  • Do not throw waste in open areas
  • Do not touch face with dirty hands
  • Do not ignore personal hygiene
  • Do not share personal items like towels

🧼 About Sanitization

Sanitization is the process of removing germs, dirt, and harmful bacteria from surfaces, objects, and environment to maintain safety and hygiene.

🧴 About Hygiene

Hygiene means maintaining cleanliness in daily life to prevent diseases and keep ourselves healthy physically and mentally.

🚿 Personal Hygiene

  • Bathing daily
  • Brushing teeth twice a day
  • Washing hands regularly
  • Wearing clean clothes

🌸 Menstrual Hygiene

Maintaining cleanliness during menstruation using sanitary pads, proper disposal, and regular washing is very important for health and safety.

🧼 How to Be Sanitized

  • Wash hands with soap for 20 seconds
  • Use sanitizer before eating or after touching surfaces
  • Clean frequently touched objects
  • Keep surroundings clean and dry

🔍 How to Check Hygiene

  • Check cleanliness of hands and nails
  • Check surroundings (no waste or smell)
  • Check water quality and cleanliness
  • Ensure personal cleanliness habits

💡 Caring Tips

  • Drink clean and safe water
  • Maintain daily hygiene routine
  • Avoid touching face with dirty hands
  • Keep environment clean
