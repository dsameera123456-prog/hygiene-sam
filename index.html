Hygiene Connect & Sanitization Care

A community service project focusing on awareness of cleanliness, hygiene habits, and disease prevention in society.

🧼 About Sanitization

Sanitization removes germs and keeps surroundings safe and clean.

🧴 About Hygiene

Hygiene means maintaining cleanliness to prevent diseases.

🚿 Personal Hygiene

  • Bathing daily
  • Brushing teeth twice
  • Washing hands regularly
  • Wearing clean clothes

🌸 Menstrual Hygiene

Proper use of sanitary pads and cleanliness during menstruation.

💡 Caring Tips

  • Drink clean water
  • Keep environment clean
  • Avoid dirty hands on face

📸 Demo Image Process

Hand washing, sanitizing, cleaning surfaces, waste disposal.

🏆 Our Work

Awareness programs and hygiene activities conducted by our team.

📊 Outcome

  • Improved hygiene awareness
  • Reduced disease spread
  • Better cleanliness habits

🎯 Project Aim

To spread awareness about hygiene and sanitization.

📈 Project Result

Successfully improved hygiene awareness in community.
